## 프롬프트 버전 1: 신뢰감 중심 (추천)

```
Create a professional and trustworthy 30-second video advertisement for a Korean medical health screening service called "찾아검진" (Chajagumjin).

**Visual Style:**
- Clean, modern medical aesthetic with soft, calming colors
- Primary brand colors: teal green (#2F9A88) and turquoise (#4BBEAC)
- Professional lighting, crisp and clear imagery
- Subtle, gentle animations that convey trust and precision
- Avoid flashy or overly commercial effects

**Scene Structure (30 seconds):**

**0-5초**: Opening
- Soft fade-in showing a warm, modern medical office or home setting
- Gentle background music begins
- Text overlay: "암 조기 발견을 더 가볍게"

**5-12초**: Problem Statement
- Show a person looking concerned or thoughtful (stock footage)
- Smooth transition to medical imagery (blood sample tube, lab equipment)
- Text overlay: "복잡한 검진, 시간 부족으로 미루셨나요?"

**12-20초**: Solution Introduction
- Show professional healthcare worker gently performing blood draw in a comfortable setting
- Close-up of blood sample being collected (clean, sterile, professional)
- Text overlay: "집이나 회사에서 15분 채혈로"
- Smooth animation showing the liquid biopsy concept (blood → DNA analysis visualization)

**20-26초**: Technology & Trust
- Show advanced lab equipment (subtle, not overwhelming)
- Display visual representation of NGS+AI analysis (abstract, elegant data visualization)
- Text overlay: "액체생검 기술로 8대 암종을 정확하게"
- Include subtle trust indicators: medical certifications, professional imagery

**26-30초**: Call to Action
- Return to warm, approachable setting
- Clear logo reveal: "찾아검진"
- Text overlay: "지금 예약하기"
- Website URL or contact information appears smoothly

**Tone & Mood:**
- Calm, reassuring, and professional
- Emphasize safety, accuracy, and convenience
- Warm but authoritative
- Avoid fear-based messaging; focus on empowerment and early detection

**Text Guidelines:**
- Use clean, readable Korean typography
- Sans-serif font, professional and modern
- Subtle shadows or outlines for readability
- Smooth fade-in/fade-out animations for text

**Audio:**
- Soft, calming background music (instrumental, healthcare/wellness genre)
- No harsh sounds or sudden volume changes
- Professional voiceover (optional): warm, clear Korean female or male voice

**Technical Requirements:**
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- High resolution (1080p minimum)
- Smooth transitions between scenes
- Professional color grading
- Optimized for Facebook feed playback
```

## 프롬프트 버전 2: 편의성 강조

```
Create a 30-second Facebook video ad for "찾아검진" (Chajagumjin), a Korean liquid biopsy health screening service.

**Core Message**: "암 조기 발견을 더 가볍게 - 집이나 회사에서 15분 채혈로"

**Visual Narrative:**

**Scene 1 (0-7초)**: Modern Lifestyle
- Show a busy professional person at home or office
- Clean, bright modern Korean interior
- Person looks at calendar/phone with a "busy schedule" visual cue
- Text: "바쁜 일상, 검진은 언제 하시나요?"

**Scene 2 (7-15초)**: Convenience Highlight
- Healthcare professional arrives at home/office (warm, friendly appearance)

**Scene 3 (15-23초)**: Technology Showcase
- Elegant visualization of liquid biopsy process

- Text: "액체생검 기술로 8대 암종 검사"

**Scene 4 (23-30초)**: Trust & Action
- Show results/report being delivered (digital, modern)

- Clean, minimal design

## 프롬프트 버전 3: 기술력 강조 (과학적 신뢰)

```
Create a professional 30-second medical technology video for "찾아검진" liquid biopsy service.

**Focus**: Emphasize scientific accuracy and advanced technology while maintaining approachability.

**Visual Flow:**

**0-8초**: Human Touch
- Warm opening: Person receiving gentle care from healthcare professional
- Emphasis on comfort and professionalism
- Text: "암 조기 발견, 이제 더 쉽게"

**8-18초**: Technology Deep Dive
- Smooth transition to scientific visualization
- Elegant animation showing liquid biopsy process:
  * Blood sample collection (professional, sterile)
  * DNA extraction visualization (abstract, beautiful)
  * NGS sequencing representation (data streams, elegant)
  * AI analysis visualization (neural network, modern)
- Text: "NGS+AI 액체생검 기술"

**18-25초**: Results & Trust
- Show comprehensive test coverage (8 cancer types)
- Display professional medical report (clean, readable)
- Include subtle trust markers (lab certifications, professional standards)
- Text: "8대 암종 정밀 분석"

**25-30초**: Brand & CTA
- Logo reveal with gradient from #2F9A88 to #4BBEAC
- Modern, clean typography
- Text: "찾아검진 - 지금 예약하기"
- Contact information

**Technical Style:**
- High-end medical documentary aesthetic
- Precise, clean visuals
- Professional color grading
- Subtle particle effects for scientific elements
- Smooth camera movements
- Emphasis on accuracy and precision

**Avoid**: Overly technical jargon visuals, fear-based imagery, cheap effects

## 추가 고려사항

### 법적/윤리적 주의사항
- 의료 광고 규정 준수
- 과장된 표현 금지
- 치료 효과 보장 표현 금지
- 정확한 정보 전달

### 플랫폼 최적화
- **Facebook Feed**: 16:9 또는 1:1 비율
- **Facebook Stories**: 9:16 수직 형식
- 첫 3초가 중요 (자동 재생 시 무음)
- 텍스트는 이미지의 20% 이하 권장

### 신뢰감 강화 요소
- 의료진의 전문성 표현
- 인증된 시설/장비 시각화
- 명확한 개인정보 보호 언급
- 고객 만족도/리뷰 (허용된 경우)

### A/B 테스트 추천
- 버전 1 (신뢰감 중심) vs 버전 4 (감성적 접근)
- CTA 텍스트 변화
- 음악 스타일 비교
